Honest

The debut Album from Luke Jenison aka Your Mother’s Favorite Rapper. Originally planned as the first and last project from our aforementioned hero, it ended up being just the beginning of a long and prosperous journey. Whether it’s the groovy rhythm and flows of “Good Girl” or relentless delivery of bars on “Luke Skywalker Freestyle”, Honest has a little something for every occasion.
